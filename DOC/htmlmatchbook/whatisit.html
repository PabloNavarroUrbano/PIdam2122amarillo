MatchBook

MatchBook is an APP made for regular readers and not so regular readers, from people who read everyday to people that have never read an entire book.

In this APP you will find a multitude of fragments from books that you may never have read and fall in love while reading them, you can share with the rest of the world fragments that you like or discover new ones that other people uploaded.
